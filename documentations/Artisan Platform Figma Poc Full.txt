Artisan Market Platform – Full Figma POC Specification & JSON Blueprint
Objective
This document contains the complete Figma-ready POC specification for the Artisan Market Platform, covering all screens, components, interactions, and visual style for both Leather Shoes and Bhadohi Carpets industries across all three phases (Onboarding, Product/Market, Production Input). The JSON blueprint can be used to import into Figma or guide designers and developers directly.

1. Pages / Screens
Phase 1: Artisan Onboarding & Profile
	•	Welcome Screen
	•	Industry selection dropdown (Leather / Bhadohi Carpets)
	•	CTA: “Start Onboarding”
	•	User Registration
	•	Individual / Company toggle
	•	Name, Email, Mobile fields with inline validation
	•	Tooltip guidance for GST/Tax/UDYAM registration
	•	Compliance Verification
	•	Status indicators: Verified / Pending / Not Registered
	•	“Guide Me” workflow
	•	Bank Details Integration
	•	Dropdown for pre-integrated banks
	•	Account Number, IFSC fields
	•	Verification success indicator
	•	Profile Summary
	•	Artisan profile overview
	•	Phase 1 schemes applied (MSME etc.)
	•	Editable fields CTA
Phase 2: Product & Market Layer
	•	Product Selection Screen
	•	Product cards for Leather Shoes and Bhadohi Carpets
	•	Dynamic Configurator Forms
	•	Leather Shoes: Material, Size, Color, Stitching Type, Design Complexity, Custom Design Option
	•	Bhadohi Carpets: Material, Length, Width, Weave Type, Custom Design Option
	•	Conditional fields based on premium/standard tier
	•	Market Intelligence Panel
	•	Demand index (gauge chart)
	•	Trend analysis (line chart)
	•	Eligible markets (color-coded list)
	•	Phase 2 scheme overlay badges
	•	Pricing Tier Panel
	•	Premium / Standard dynamic suggestion
	•	Scheme-adjusted pricing visualization
Phase 3: Production Input & Submission
	•	Production Input Forms
	•	Leather Shoes: Shoe Type, Quantity, Stitching Type, Packaging Options
	•	Bhadohi Carpets: Weave Type, Dimensions, Quantity, Packaging Options
	•	Conditional fields based on tier and scheme eligibility
	•	Validation & Preview
	•	Inline validation messages
	•	Summary of market intelligence + applied schemes
	•	Submission Confirmation
	•	Success message with next steps
	•	CTA: “Go to Dashboard”
Dashboards
	•	Artisan Overview: Profile status, compliance, bank info, schemes applied
	•	Market Insights: Demand trends, pricing suggestions, eligible markets
	•	Notifications: Alerts for schemes, validation, submission status
Admin Panel (Optional POC Integration)
	•	Dynamic field control
	•	Scheme overlay management
	•	Price modifier control

2. Components
	•	Buttons: Primary, Secondary, Disabled, Hover
	•	Input Fields: Text, Number, Dropdown, Checkbox, Radio, Date, File Upload
	•	Alerts: Success, Error, Info, Warning
	•	Cards: Product cards, Market intelligence cards, Scheme badges
	•	Modals: Help/Info tooltips, Terms & Conditions
	•	Dashboard Widgets: Market overview, Production summary, Scheme notifications

3. Design Tokens
	•	Colors: Primary (#1D4ED8), Secondary (#2563EB), Accent (#FBBF24), Background (#F9FAFB), Error (#DC2626)
	•	Typography: Headings - Inter Bold, Body - Inter Regular, Caption - Inter Medium
	•	Spacing: Grid 8px, Padding 16px, Margin 16px
	•	Icons & Illustrations: Industry-specific icons, conditional indicators

4. Interactions / UX Notes
	•	Conditional field visibility based on tier, product type, and scheme eligibility
	•	Step progress indicator for multi-step forms
	•	Hover & focus states for input and buttons
	•	Dynamic preview panel for product & market data
	•	Toast notifications for validation and submission feedback
